What Makes Our Chai Special?

Chai, Which Is Often Pronounced “Chee-Ah,"

Is A Spiced Black Tea Usually Served With Added Sugar And Milk That Is Usually Consumed In The Winter Months In India. The Basic Ingredients Of Chai Include Black Tea, Sugar, Milk, And Other Spices. However, Chai Is Much More Than Just The Ingredients Listed Above. The Taste And Texture Of Chai Are Determined By A Wide Range Of Factors, Including, But Not Limited To The Weather, The Season, The Time Of Year, And The Strength Of The Drinker’s Taste. While Not Everyone Finds Chai Relaxing, It Is Meant To Promote Rest And Reduce Stress.
